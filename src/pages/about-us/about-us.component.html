About the Faculty of Science

Dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in the scientific disciplines

Our Mission

Advancing Scientific Knowledge and Education

The Faculty of Science of Sfax is committed to providing high-quality education in various scientific disciplines, conducting cutting-edge research, and contributing to the advancement of knowledge and innovation.

We strive to prepare our students for successful careers in science and technology by equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and experiences needed to excel in a rapidly evolving global landscape.

Our Vision

Shaping the Future Through Science

We envision the Faculty of Science of Sfax as a leading institution in scientific education and research, recognized nationally and internationally for its academic excellence, innovative research, and societal impact.

Our goal is to foster a vibrant academic community that embraces diversity, encourages critical thinking, and promotes collaboration across disciplines to address complex scientific challenges and contribute to sustainable development.
